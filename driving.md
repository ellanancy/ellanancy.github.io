# Driving Rules

## GTA Driving

- Drive in a way that respects the class of vehicle you are using.
- All vehicles may drive on tracks, but only off-road vehicles may climb smooth, steep hills.
- Clever use of environment (small ramps, gaps) is allowed.
- Extreme or unrealistic environment usage is not permitted.
- You cannot continue to drive a vehicle that has lost 3+ tyres in PvP RP.

## General Driving

- Drive in a realistic manner at all times.
- No large ramps or unrealistic jumps; small gaps/curbs/ledges are acceptable.
- If your car rolls multiple times, you must RP the damage/situation.
- Only backseat passengers may carry others; driver and front passenger cannot carry.
