# General Rules

## Basic Requirements

- A working quality microphone is required.
- Twitch TOS must be followed at all times.
- This is an English-speaking server; you are expected to communicate in English.
- Real-world current affairs and tragedies must not be brought into RP.
- Unity RP has a zero-tolerance policy on discrimination and trolling.

## Character Rules

- Characters must be completely fictional and must not represent a version of yourself.
- Characters must have realistic names that follow Twitch TOS.
- Characters must have all four limbs attached.

## Mental Health & Sensitive RP

- Mental health, self-harm, and suicide roleplay are **not permitted**.
- Use of immersion-breaking terms and OOC communication in city is forbidden.
