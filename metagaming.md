# Metagaming

Metagaming is using information your character does **not** have, gained outside their in-character experiences.

- Do not use Discord, streams, clips, or OOC chat to gain IC advantages.
- Staff may punish metagaming even if evidence is incomplete but strongly indicative.
- Proof must be presented by you, not by other people’s streams/clips.
