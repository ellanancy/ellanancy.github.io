# Powergaming / FailRP

Powergaming is using mechanics or abilities outside normal GTA/FiveM function, or forcing RP to a desired outcome with no counterplay.

## Examples

- Using `/me` to gain unreasonable advantage (e.g. `/me holds knife to neck` to force outcomes).
- Forcing players to withdraw money/items under threat; you may rob on-hand cash only.
- Cancelling emotes/animations during PvP to gain advantage.
- RP’ing actions while doing impossible actions (e.g. carrying someone while driving).
- Exploiting mechanics (start robbery, leave, return later for rewards).
- Calling out locations while downed.
- Using respawn while others are still active on scene.
- Escorting a player who is not cuffed or downed to remove their counterplay.
