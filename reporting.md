# Player Reports & Compensation

## Player Reports

- All reports via Discord ticket.
- Must include evidence (preferably video with audio and IDs).
- Minimum 2-minute clip with full audio leading up to rule break.
- Reports must be submitted within 24 hours of the incident.
- No audio = no action. No clip = no action.

## Compensation (Comps)

- Unity RP runs a **no-clip, no-comp** policy with no exceptions.

## Spite Reporting

- Reporting someone purely because you/others were punished for similar actions may be classed as spite reporting.
