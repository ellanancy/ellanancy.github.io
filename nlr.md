# New Life Rule

When you respawn, your character forgets all circumstances surrounding their death.

- You may not ask how you “woke up with a headache”.
- If you receive hospital treatment from a downed state, you cannot return to that scene.
- PD may return to an active scene after 1 hour; they cannot return to a raid until it fully concludes.
