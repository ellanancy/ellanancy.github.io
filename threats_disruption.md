# Threats & Disruption / Knowledge of Rule Breaks

Players must be positive assets to the community.

You may **not**:

- Engage in or ignore doxxing/harassment of community or staff.
- Engage in or ignore cyber threats to server, players, or staff.
- Engage in or ignore use of 3rd party cheats.

Severe rule breaks (cheating, ban evasion, major metagaming, harassment, cyber threats, malicious intent) must be reported. Failing to report can make you complicit.
