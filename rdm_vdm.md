# Random Death Match (RDM) & Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

## Random Death Match (RDM)

- There must be valid roleplay reasoning before engaging in hostile RP.
- Players must be given the opportunity to respond to the interaction.
- RDM is judged case-by-case; poor RP reasoning will be punished as RDM.

## Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

VDM is defined as using a vehicle to kill, hurt, or damage another player or their property without prior initiation or valid RP reason.

### Examples of VDM

- Intentionally ramming another vehicle.
- Nudging a vehicle to knock a fleeing player over.
- Knocking down or killing another player intentionally using your vehicle.
- Excessive ramming.

**Exception:** If your life is in danger and a vehicle is blocking your only reasonable escape route.
